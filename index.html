Our Studio

Unwind and step into a haven of tranquility at Santanna Hair Studio. We believe beauty thrives in a space that caters not just to your outer appearance, but also to your inner peace. Our passionate stylists listen intently to understand your vision and craft a personalized experience that leaves you feeling refreshed and radiant.

Indulge in our curated selection of luxurious treatments designed to revitalize your mind, body, and spirit. Whether you seek a transformative haircut, a deeply nourishing hair treatment, or a touch of makeup artistry, our team is dedicated to helping you discover and embrace your most confident and beautiful self.

Services

We offer a range of services, including:

  • Blow Drying

    Our blowdry service is a perfect way to achieve a polished and manageable style. Whether you have short hair or hair reaching your shoulders, our stylists can create a smooth, sleek finish that flatters your features. We also offer additional options for longer hair and those seeking extra volume or texture.

  • Colour & Highlights

    Transform your hair with our extensive range of coloring options! We offer full head highlights for a complete color change, or a full tint for a rich, all-over shade. For added dimension, consider full tint with floodlights or a T-bar of highlights. We also specialize in half head highlights for a more subtle effect, and masking treatments to deeply nourish your hair. Need to refresh your color? O ur toner service can revive your existing highlights or tint. Wella color options are also available for those seeking a trusted brand.

  • Styling

    We offer a range of cutting services for the whole family. Keep your little ones looking their best with our children's cuts for boys and girls under 12. Men can choose our dedicated gents cut for a clean and stylish look. And for a complete hair transformation, our ladies cut and blow dry service combines a precision haircut with a polished blow dry finish.

  • Treatments

    Nourish and revitalize your hair with our range of in-salon treatments. We offer a glossing treatment for added shine and vibrancy, a hair masque for deep hydration, and Olaplex treatments to strengthen hair bonds and improve manageability. Whether you choose a standalone Olaplex treatment or combine it with a color service, your hair will be left feeling healthy and looking its best.
